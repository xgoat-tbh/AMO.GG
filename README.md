# AMO.GG 🎮

> The official Discord bot for the **AMO INDIA** server — built to keep things running smoothly so you can focus on having fun.

---

## What is AMO.GG?

AMO.GG is a custom-built Discord bot made specifically for the AMO INDIA community. It handles the behind-the-scenes work that keeps a server organized — managing voice channels, assigning roles, coordinating games, and keeping the community experience consistent for everyone.

Think of it as the server's silent staff member that's always online.

---

## What Can It Do?

### 🔊 Voice Moderation
Keeps voice channels clean and controlled. Whether that's managing who can join, handling disruptions, or automating common voice-related tasks — AMO.GG has it covered without needing a mod to step in every time.

### 🏷️ Role Management
Automatically handles role assignments so members get the right access at the right time. No more waiting for an admin to give you a role — the bot takes care of it.

### 🎮 Game Coordination
Makes it easier to organize gaming sessions within the server. Whether you're looking for teammates, scheduling matches, or just keeping game-related activity in order, AMO.GG helps bring the community together around games.

### 🏘️ Community Systems
The backbone of the server experience — from welcoming new members to keeping things structured as the community grows. AMO.GG handles the recurring tasks that would otherwise fall on mods and admins.
